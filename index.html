What's an "AI agent", really?

There's a big difference between a chatbot that talks and an agent that gets work done. Here's the plain-English version.

Chatbot

Answers questions

  • Replies with scripted or generated text
  • Waits for you to ask
  • Can't take real action in your systems
Rule-based automation

Follows fixed rules

  • Great for simple "if this, then that"
  • Breaks when the situation is unexpected
  • Can't reason or handle nuance
AI agent

Understands & takes action

  • Works out what needs to happen
  • Pulls info from your systems and acts
  • Escalates to a human when it should
